About Me

I am a Co-founder and CTO of a private research and development company called NERD. I have a background in Aeronautical Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Mechatronics. My mission is to build a technically competent community of researchers and developers that firmly believe in open source philosophy. I have experience in organizing seminars, short-term training, hackathons, and technical meetups in different parts of Ethiopia. I strongly believes in pan-African technology integration. I'm an NVIDIA Jetson Ambassador, a trainer, mentor, and Rotarian.
