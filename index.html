Tech Camp Ethiopia 2020

is a series of programs that encourage all of youths - makers, engineers, do-gooders, executives, computer scientists, inventors, innovators - who are making things that are not just nice to have, but that people need.

Each program is involves 20 participants and takes a maximum of 8 days for one-to-one workshops, followed by project-based challenges completed within a month.

The Programs

Each program/cohort is designed to bring 20 young ethusiatsts with similar intersts and charachterstics.
For each cohort, we have the following topics to cover in the Tech Camp
Mobile App Cohort
The Mobile App cohort is hands-on experiences to designing and bulding mobile applications using Flutter. This cohort enourages the participants to learn by building increasingly more sophisticated and meaningful mobile applications for both Andriod and IOS with one single codebase.

By the end of the course, each participant will build their own complete Flutter application incorporating most of the key aspects of the bootcamp materials. Participants are expected to build real-world projects at the end of this cohort.

Web Full-Stack Cohort
This Cohort will allow participants to learn the fundamentals of modern web development to build great, full-fledged websites. You will learn you how to build modern, in-demand software that runs on any browser flawlessly.

At the end of this cohort, participants will be working on real-world projects to soldify what they have undertaken at the Full Stack Cohort.

About Us

Google Developers Group (GDG) Addis is run by passionate individuals in the developer community.
Join us for an opportunity to meet local developers with similar interests in technology, attend talks on a wide range of technical topics, and learn new skills through hands-on workshops

Be part of an inclusive environment

Everyone and anyone interested in tech - from beginner developers to experienced professionals - are welcome to join. We are а group of enthusiasts that gather around Google technologies. Founded back in 2016, we organize various events - seminars, conferences or just community dine outs, and discuss the latest Google developments and Technology Trends. With over 60 events since then and nearly 2,400 community members..
